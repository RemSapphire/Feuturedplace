Tukuran Fish Port

Tukuran, Zamboanga del Sur, Philippines

Tukuran, known for its rice fields and vibrant fishing industry, is home to a thriving fish port where visitors can watch fishermen unload their daily catch, explore the seafood market, and enjoy fresh seafood dishes. Its long stretch of fine black sand also makes it a favorite weekend getaway for locals and visitors from nearby towns like Pagadian.

Travel Tips

Make the most of your visit to Tukuran with these helpful tips

Best Time to Visit

Visit during the dry season (November to May) for the best beach experience and outdoor activities.

Getting Around

Tricycles and motorcycles are the main modes of transportation within Tukuran. Consider renting for convenience.

Accommodation

Book accommodations in advance, especially during peak season and local festivals.

Local Currency

Bring cash as ATMs are limited. Most small establishments don't accept credit cards.
